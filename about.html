About

While many pay attention to federal politics, state politics generally falls under the radar. In a safe state such as Oklahoma, this is even more so. Inspired by Voteview.com, SoonerView provides a recources for Oklahomans to explore votes in the Oklahoma Legislature (including committee votes). Page designed as part of a project at Oklahoma State University for a Software Engineering Course by Jace Bormann, Aaron Daugherty, Aiden Maner, Malik Melendez, and Ryan Price.

Data for measures, votes, and legislators gathered from LegiScan Public API. © LegiScan. Licensed under CC BY 4.0.
